Let's make work enjoyable

Flexible & Remote

Flexible working patterns agreed in advance. Includes part-time hours, condensed hours, flexible start/end times and 100% remote working.

Annual Leave

25 days annual leave (plus public holidays) and no fixed holiday dates. Plus, your holiday will increase by a day for every two years you're here.

Healthcare Cashback

Free NHS cashback health plan which lets you claim cashback on a wide range of health costs.

Development Allowance

Annual allowance of £500 and 6 days leave to focus exclusively on your professional development, doing anything that helps you to thrive in your role.

Wellbeing Allowance

Monthly allowance of £50 to spend on anything that helps you feel your healthiest and best self.

Socials & Outings

We regularly get together for outings to theatres, galleries, historic sites and cultural events. Plus a range of optional social events and quarterly team lunches.

Annual Profit Share

We offer enhanced pay for new parents, so you can feel comfortable taking more time off to be with your family if you choose.

Annual Profit Share

A formalised profit share scheme that gives every Substrakt employee a share of our profits if we hit our targets.

Weekly Yoga

Half an hour every week to stretch, relax and clear your mind with other members of the team.
